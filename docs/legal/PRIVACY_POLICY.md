## Required Sections

A compliant Privacy Policy for the AI Passive Income Navigator must include:

### 1. Information We Collect
- Personal information (email, name, profile data)
- Usage data (pages visited, features used)
- Technical data (IP address, browser type, device info)
- Cookies and tracking technologies

### 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain the service
- To personalize user experience
- To send notifications and updates
- To improve our services
- To comply with legal obligations

### 3. How We Share Your Information
- With service providers (Base44, hosting, analytics)
- With AI services (OpenAI, Claude) for feature functionality
- For legal compliance
- With user consent

### 4. Data Storage and Security
- Where data is stored (Base44 platform, geographic location)
- Security measures implemented
- Data retention periods
- Encryption practices

### 5. User Rights
- Access your data
- Correct your data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data (GDPR right to portability)
- Opt-out of marketing communications

### 6. Cookies and Tracking
- Types of cookies used
- Purpose of each cookie
- How to disable cookies
- Third-party analytics (if used)

### 7. Children's Privacy
- Statement on compliance with COPPA (if applicable)
- Minimum age requirements

### 8. International Data Transfers
- GDPR compliance for EU users
- Privacy Shield or alternative frameworks
- Data transfer mechanisms

### 9. Changes to This Policy
- How users will be notified of changes
- Effective date of policy

### 10. Contact Information
- How to contact us about privacy concerns
- Data Protection Officer (if applicable)
