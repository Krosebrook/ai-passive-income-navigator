## 1. Introduction

Welcome to AI Passive Income Navigator ("the Application," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our web application. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the Application.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account and use the Application, you may provide us with:

- **Account information:** Email address and display name (collected via the Base44 authentication service)
- **Profile and preferences:** Investment preferences, risk tolerance, target income goals, and other settings you configure during onboarding
- **Portfolio data:** Information about passive income streams you track, including financial figures (income, expenses, investments)
- **Content you create:** Ideas, notes, tags, deal pipeline entries, and bookmarks you create within the Application
- **Community content:** Posts, comments, and other content you submit to the community features

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the Application, we automatically collect:

- **Usage data:** Pages visited, features used, buttons clicked, and time spent on the Application
- **Performance data:** Core Web Vitals metrics (page load speed, interaction responsiveness) to improve Application performance
- **Error data:** JavaScript errors and exceptions to identify and fix bugs
- **Device information:** Browser type, browser version, and operating system type (not individually identifying)
- **Session identifiers:** Anonymous session IDs to correlate events within a single browsing session

We do **not** collect your IP address for tracking purposes. We do not use persistent advertising trackers.

### 2.3 Information from Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

- **Base44 Platform:** Authentication, database storage, and serverless function execution. See [Section 6](#6-data-sharing-and-third-party-services).
- **AI/LLM Services (via Base44):** When you use AI-powered features (deal analysis, idea generation, market insights), your input — including deal names, descriptions, and categories — is processed by an AI language model. This processing happens through Base44's integration layer.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

- **Performance of a Contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR):** To provide you with the Application and its features
- **Legitimate Interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR):** To improve the Application, prevent fraud, and ensure security
- **Legal Obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR):** Where processing is required by applicable law
- **Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR):** Where we explicitly request your consent (e.g., optional analytics)

You may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

## 6. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

### 6.1 Base44 Platform

The Application is built on Base44 (BaaS — Backend as a Service). Base44 stores and processes:

- Your account credentials (email, hashed password)
- All data you enter into the Application (portfolio items, ideas, deals, preferences)
- Usage analytics and error logs

**Base44's Privacy Policy:** [https://base44.com/privacy] — review before using the Application.

We rely on Base44's data processing agreement for GDPR compliance purposes.

### 6.2 AI Language Model Services

When you use AI-powered features, your input is sent to an AI language model via Base44's integration layer. The following data is processed by the AI:

- Deal names and descriptions
- Idea titles and descriptions
- Your stated investment criteria and preferences

**The AI does not receive:** Your email address, precise financial figures (unless you include them in a description), or data from other users.

### 6.3 Error Monitoring

If configured, error data (JavaScript errors, stack traces) may be sent to an error monitoring service such as Sentry. Error data does **not** include personal data from your portfolio.

### 6.4 Disclosure Required by Law

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency).

### 6.5 Business Transfers

If the Application or its operator is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified via the email address associated with your account.

We do **not** share your data with any other third parties.

## 7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### 7.1 Cookies We Use

| Cookie | Type | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|------|---------|----------|
| `base44_session` | Essential | Authentication session token | Session |
| `base44_auth` | Essential | Persistent login state | 30 days |

We use only essential cookies required for the Application to function. We do **not** use:
- Advertising or tracking cookies
- Third-party analytics cookies (e.g., Google Analytics)
- Social media tracking pixels

### 7.2 Local Storage

The Application uses browser local storage to cache application data (React Query cache). This data:
- Is stored only on your device
- Does not leave your browser except as part of normal API calls
- Is cleared when you log out or clear browser data

### 7.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in to the Application.

## 9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data:

- **Encryption in transit:** All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
- **Authentication:** JWT-based authentication via Base44's auth service
- **Access controls:** User data is scoped to individual accounts — you can only access your own data
- **Input validation:** All user inputs are validated using Zod schemas before processing
- **Dependency scanning:** Regular `npm audit` and Dependabot scans for vulnerable dependencies
- **No plaintext secrets:** API tokens and credentials are stored in environment variables, never in source code

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to [INSERT SECURITY EMAIL] following our [Responsible Disclosure Policy](../../SECURITY.md).
